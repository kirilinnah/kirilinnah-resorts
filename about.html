Discover Papua New Guinea

Papua New Guinea (PNG) sits on the eastern half of the world's second‑largest island and is home to more than 800 languages and dozens of distinct cultures. Much of the country remains wild and unexplored, with towering mountains, ancient rainforests and some of the most pristine coral reefs on Earth.

Travelers visiting PNG can expect warm hospitality, adventurous excursions, and the opportunity to learn from indigenous communities. Our collection of resorts brings you closer to nature while providing modern comforts.

Whether you're birdwatching in a cloud forest, diving among sea turtles or simply relaxing on a tropical beach, PNG resorts are the gateway to a true South Pacific paradise.
